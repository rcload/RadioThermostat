Change settings

Touch the spinners to make the corresponding changes on your thermostat. Pocket Thermostat sends a message to your thermostat and displays an error if it fails.

The only unobvious feature is that if you want the thermostat to revert to the temperature setting at the current time and day on its schedule, you need not enter a temperature. Instead, delete the temperature digits from the "Set Target" field, and select "Until scheduled" from the adjacent spinner selector.
