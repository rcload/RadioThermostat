Main

Pocket Thermostat only communicates with the your thermostat when you click buttons on your Android--never automatically. Click the Refresh button to update Pocket Thermostat's display of your thermostat state. The thermostat's web server is not especially reliable. Pocket Thermostat displays an error message when a message fails. You must click its button again to try again.

The Android Menu on the main screen gives access to the Celsius/Fahrenheit unit selection.
