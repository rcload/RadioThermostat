Download and Install

Pocket Thermostat is not currently on the Android Market. Radiothermostat.com does not allow charging for such an application without prior permission according to their terms of use for their API. (Their lawyers are particularly threatening: "...may render you liable for damages and injunctive relief in a court of law.")

Always use caution installing any application on your Android phone. You should find some assurance that the application does not, for example, share your private data without your consent. In the case of PocketThermostat, this is some supporting information:
  • When installing, Pocket Thermostat only requests that you authorize internet access and no other Android privileges.

Install

To install this app on your Android phone, you must
  • Use the Android Settings menu, go to Applications, and make "Unknown Sources" checked on to allow installation of non-Market applications.
  • Your Android Browser must have Javascript enabled in order to download.
  • While running the Browser application on your Android, come back to this page you are reading and then click this button and agree to my terms of use:

  • In the Android Browser, once the download is finished, click its Menu key, choose More, and then Downloads. That brings you this screen:
    or a similar one. Click on PocketThermostat... to install.

If you have previously configured your thermostat to communicate with radiothermostat.com according to their installation instructions, there is no requirement to disconnect that connection to their cloud software. However, it is very unlikely you got this far in this document if you wanted to use both Pocket Thermostat and radiothermostat together.
