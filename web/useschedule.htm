Edit Schedule

The schedule fits better in landscape mode as shown below, but also can be edited in portrait mode. Wipe the screen left and right to scroll the days of week into view. Here is the sequence Pocket Thermostat is designed to edit:
  • click the "Copy from thermostat" button to retrieve the thermostat's schedule.
  • make your edits,
  • use the Android Menu button to access the "Copy to thermostat" button.
Note that Pocket Thermostat only attempts to transact to or from the thermostat once per button click. It puts a message on the screen indicating if it fails--which the thermostat often does. Click the button again until the error message goes away.

The Heating Schedule is copied and edited indepently from the Cooling Schedule.
